12 Free Tools - Everything in One Place

1. AI Mood Quote Generator 🎯

What is this: Get instant inspirational quotes based on your mood.
How it works: Select your mood and click generate. Perfect for WhatsApp status, Instagram posts and daily motivation.

2. Name Meaning + Lucky Number ✨

What is this: Find your name meaning, lucky color and personality.
How it works: Just type your name and click button. Perfect for sharing with friends and knowing fun facts about yourself.

3. To-Do List 📝

What is this: A simple list to write your daily tasks.
How it works: Type your work and click Add. Stay organized and never forget important work. Delete tasks when completed.

    4. Calculator 🧮

    What is this: A basic calculator for addition and subtraction.
    How it works: Enter two numbers and click Add or Minus. Quickly calculate bills, expenses or any math problems.

    5. Language Translator 🌍

    What is this: Translate Urdu, Hindi, Arabic to English instantly.
    How it works: Type any language text and click translate. Great for assignments, emails and chatting with international friends.

    6. Photo Editor 🖼️

    What is this: Edit your photos with 1-click filters.
    How it works: Upload photo and click filter buttons. Make your photos Black & White, Bright or Blur for social media posts.

    7. Password Generator 🔑

    What is this: Generate strong and secure passwords instantly.
    How it works: Click the button and get 12 character strong password. Keep your Facebook, Gmail and Bank accounts safe from hackers.

    8. BMI Calculator 💪

    What is this: Check your health using weight and height.
    How it works: Enter weight in KG and height in CM. Find out if your weight is normal, underweight or overweight.

    9. Notes App 📒

    What is this: A place to save your thoughts and important info.
    How it works: Write notes and click Save. Your notes will be saved even after closing. Click Load to see them again.

    10. Currency Converter 💵

    What is this: Convert USD to Pakistani Rupees.
    How it works: Enter USD amount and click convert. Useful for online shopping and calculating money transfers.

    *Rate: 1 USD = 280 PKR Approx

    11. QR Code Generator 📱

    What is this: Create QR Codes for any link or text.
    How it works: Type link or text and click generate. Share your WhatsApp, YouTube channel or website link easily via QR.

    12. Age Calculator 🎂

    What is this: Calculate your exact age from Date of Birth.
    How it works: Select your DOB and click calculate. Perfect for forms, birthdays and having fun with friends.
